    What happens

    A recorded lesson uploads. The pipeline computes a SHA-256 checksum, files it under a job ID, and queues the job. The team sees a tracking link immediately.

    Why it matters

    Without a checksum, the same lesson gets re-processed every time someone re-uploads. Without a queue, the reviewer sits not knowing whether the job started. Both costs add up across thirty lessons a week.
