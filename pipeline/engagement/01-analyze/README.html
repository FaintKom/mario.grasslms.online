Phase 1 · Analyse · Overview

Intent

Convert the engagement brief (case-study-tz.md v1.0) into structured analysis artefacts that force every downstream design choice to cite evidence. Phase 1 answers four questions before a single learning outcome is written:

  1. What is the business actually buying? (business-goal-analysis.md)
  2. Who decides, who blocks, who learns? (stakeholder-map.md, learner-personas.md, learner-journey-map.md)
  3. What is the work — at the grain of a single cold call? (task-inventory.md, cognitive-load-analysis.md)
  4. Where is the gap, and is training the right intervention? (gap-analysis.md, kirkpatrick-l4-l1-cascade.md)

The phase deliberately keeps Action Mapping (Moore) ahead of 4C/ID (Van Merriënboer): we first separate training-fixable from environment-fixable (the comp plan, lead quality, and manager-cadence variance K.M. flagged in §8), then we sequence the training that remains.
