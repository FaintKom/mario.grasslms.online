        Presentation generator

        What it does

        A short JSON brief (topic, grade, slide count, brand) feeds a four-step pipeline that produces a fully branded slide deck. The instructional designer reviews the deck — they do not open PowerPoint.

        Why it matters

        A typical deck takes the team two hours of slide work for content the designer already wrote in their head. Compressing that to fifteen minutes means the team ships eight times more decks with the same headcount.
